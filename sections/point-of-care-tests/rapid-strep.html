QC Documentation

Placeholder Where and how to record QC results.

Tip: Proper swab technique is critical for test accuracy. Swab both tonsillar pillars and posterior pharynx firmly. Avoid touching tongue, cheeks, or teeth.
  1. Placeholder Verify test kit is within expiry and packaging is intact.
  2. Placeholder Don appropriate PPE (gloves, consider mask/eye protection).
  3. Placeholder Prepare extraction tube: add specified drops of Reagent A, then Reagent B.
  4. Placeholder Instruct patient to open mouth wide; use tongue depressor.
  5. Placeholder Swab posterior pharynx and both tonsillar pillars firmly with sterile swab.
  6. Placeholder Insert swab into extraction tube; rotate and press against sides for specified time.
  7. Placeholder Remove swab, pressing against tube to extract liquid.
  8. Placeholder Apply specified drops of extracted solution to test device sample well.
  9. Placeholder Start timer; wait specified time for result.
  10. Placeholder Read result at specified time (do not read after maximum time).
  11. Placeholder Verify control line is present.
  12. Placeholder Record result immediately.
  13. Placeholder Dispose of all materials in biohazard waste.

Test Limitations

  • Placeholder Does not detect Group C or G Streptococcus.
  • Placeholder Sensitivity lower than throat culture (false negatives possible).
  • Placeholder Does not differentiate acute infection from carrier state.
  • Placeholder Additional limitations to be added.
