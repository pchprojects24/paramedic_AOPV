Water Testing

Potable water quality monitoring and testing procedures

The shipboard paramedic is responsible for routine testing of potable water to ensure it meets health and safety standards. This section links to the detailed water testing reference materials. Content pending
About Water Testing Potable water quality testing is a critical preventive health measure aboard naval vessels. Regular bacteriological and chemical testing ensures the ship's water supply is safe for consumption, cooking, and medical use. The paramedic performs routine testing on a set schedule and documents results for the Medical Officer and command.
