Available Cartridges

  • CHEM8+ Basic metabolic panel — Na, K, Cl, TCO2, BUN, Glucose, Creatinine, iCa, Hct, Hgb (calc), AnGap (calc).
  • CG4+ Blood gas and lactate — pH, PCO2, PO2, Lactate, BE (calc), HCO3 (calc), TCO2 (calc), sO2 (calc).
  • PT/INR Coagulation — Prothrombin Time (PT), International Normalised Ratio (INR).
  • Troponin I Cardiac marker — cardiac Troponin I (cTnI) for assessment of myocardial injury.
