Troubleshooting

Common problems and quick fixes before calling for support.

  • Cartridge temperature/condensation error: Warm sealed cartridge to room temp 5–10 minutes; do not hand-rub; use a fresh pouch if foil was wet or damaged.
  • Insufficient sample / 405-407 errors: Mix sample gently, discard first drop, fill smoothly to the line without bubbles; replace cartridge if partially filled.
  • Air in blood gas sample: Expel all air from syringe, keep sample capped, analyze within minutes; redraw if large bubbles remain.
  • Analyzer will not power or holds charge poorly: Reseat in dock, verify mains power, clean contacts, or swap to spare battery if available.
  • Unexpected result compared to clinical picture: Repeat with a new cartridge, confirm patient ID/sample type, and check QC status; cross-check with manual glucose/SpO₂/venous gas if available.
  • QC lockout: Complete required QC (simulator or liquid), confirm date/time, and ensure correct lot numbers are entered.

Stop and escalate

  • Repeated QC failures across two cartridges or lots.
  • Physical damage, saltwater intrusion, or persistent cartridge errors after cleaning and power cycling.
  • Consistent bias across multiple patients (e.g., all sodium low); switch to alternate testing and report to chain/biomed.
