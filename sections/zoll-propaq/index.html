Transcutaneous Pacing

  1. Placeholder — Confirm indication (symptomatic bradycardia, heart block)
  2. Placeholder — Apply multifunction pads (anterior-posterior preferred)

  5. Placeholder — Increase output (mA) until electrical capture observed

Placeholder — Caution notes for pacing: patient discomfort, movement artefact mimicking capture, verify with pulse check.
