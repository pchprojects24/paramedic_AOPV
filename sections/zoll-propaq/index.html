ZOLL Propaq MD

Quick-start monitor/defibrillator setup and menu cues for first-time users. Follow local protocols.

Quick startup (about 2-3 minutes)

  1. Power and readiness: Connect to AC or confirm two charged batteries, then press On/Standby and allow the self-test to finish. Verify date/time.
  2. Patient setup: From Home or Setup, pick the correct patient category so alarms scale correctly. Attach ECG leads or multifunction pads, choose the lead, and confirm a stable tracing.
  3. Monitoring basics: Attach SpO2 sensor and NIBP cuff. Press NIBP for a manual reading; in the NIBP menu pick an Auto interval (e.g., 15 min) or STAT for an immediate cycle. Start EtCO2 if available.
  4. Alarms: Press Alarms, highlight the parameter, and use the knob to adjust high/low limits. Set alarm volume so it is audible; Alarm Silence only pauses temporarily.

Common menu changes

  • NIBP cycle: Press the NIBP softkey, choose Manual, Auto interval, or STAT. Let the cuff fully deflate before retrying.
  • Alarm limits and volume: Alarms key → select ECG/SpO2/NIBP → adjust limits with the knob. Volume is under Alarm Volume or Setup.
  • Display/brightness: Display key to change layout/brightness; Size cycles waveform size.
  • Patient and units: Home/Setup → Patient to change adult/peds/neonate profile and weight units; confirm time/date and default NIBP interval.
  • Printing/records: Record starts/stops the strip. A 12-lead prints automatically after acquisition completes.

Defibrillation or synchronized cardioversion

  1. Apply multifunction pads; keep ECG monitoring active.
  2. Press Defib, select energy with the selector knob (enable Sync for cardioversion and confirm R markers).
  3. Charge; announce "clear"; press Shock when charged (or deliver on the R wave with Sync).
  4. Resume compressions immediately and reassess rhythm per protocol.

Pacing

  1. Press Pacer, choose Demand or Fixed mode.
  2. Set Rate and Current (mA) with the selector; increase until capture, then reduce slightly for comfort.
  3. Press Start/Stop Pacing to toggle therapy; monitor ECG and blood pressure.

Troubleshooting quick hits

  • No ECG trace: verify lead cable and electrode contact, change the selected lead, and increase waveform size/brightness.
  • NIBP failed: check cuff size/placement, keep the limb still, wait for full deflation, then retry Manual or STAT.
  • Battery low: swap in a charged pack and stay on AC/DC; keep two batteries installed.
  • No SpO2/EtCO2: reseat or replace the sensor/line; dry or replace if contaminated.
