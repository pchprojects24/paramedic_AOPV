NAS Immunization Schedule

Standard NAS immunization schedule. Always confirm current CF H Svcs Gp directives — schedules may change with new NACI recommendations or operational requirements.

Routine NAS Immunizations

Vaccine Dose / Route Schedule

Documentation Requirements

  • All immunizations must be recorded in the member's CF 2034 (Immunization Record) and the CFHIS electronic health record.
  • Lot number, site of injection, route, dose, date, and immunizer identity must be documented for every vaccine administered.
  • Adverse events must be reported using the AEFI (Adverse Event Following Immunization) form and entered into CFHIS.
  • Members should carry their yellow WHO International Certificate of Vaccination for international travel.
