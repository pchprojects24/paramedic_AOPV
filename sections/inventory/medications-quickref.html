Medications Quick Reference

Paramedic-level medication dosing, indications, and contraindications

This page provides a quick-reference guide to medications carried aboard, organized by category. All sections below are framework headings for AI (Codex) content population. Content pending
How to Use This Reference This is a rapid-access reference for paramedic-level medication administration. Each category contains a table with medication name, standard dose, route, indications, contraindications, and notes. Always verify dosing against the current protocol book and consult the MO when in doubt. This reference does NOT replace clinical judgment or standing orders.
Scope of Practice: Only administer medications within your authorized scope of practice and as directed by standing orders, medical directives, or MO verbal/written orders.
