Water Colorimeter — Hach DR900

Handheld multiparameter colorimeter for water testing with pre-programmed methods.

Quick start

  1. Install charged batteries or connect external power. Clean and dry sample cells.
  2. Power on and select the program number for the desired test (or choose from Favorites).
  3. Prepare the blank per the method, wipe the cuvette, align the mark, and press Zero.
  4. Prepare the sample with reagents/timing as directed, wipe and align the cuvette, then press Read to obtain the result.
  5. Record/store the reading; dispose of reagents safely and rinse cells immediately.

Common functions

  • Save results with sample ID and export via USB; review stored data in the log.
  • Use the built-in timer for method steps; enable backlight in low light.
  • Calibrate/verify with standards or check solutions per quality plan.

Troubleshooting

  • Out-of-range/ERR: Verify the correct program/reagents, re-zero with a fresh blank, and ensure timing is accurate.
  • Poor repeatability: Clean cuvettes and sample well, remove bubbles, and align the orientation mark consistently.
  • Low battery: Replace/charge batteries or connect external power; confirm battery contacts are clean.

Care & consumables

  • Keep the cell compartment dry; wipe spills immediately.
  • Use scratch-free, clean cuvettes; dedicate cells to specific chemistries when possible.
  • Store with the dust cap on; protect from extreme heat/cold.
