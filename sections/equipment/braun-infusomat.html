Braun Infusomat

Single-channel infusion pump (Infusomat Space family) for IV fluids, medications, and TPN.

Quick start

  1. Connect to AC power (or confirm battery charge). Gather a compatible Infusomat administration set and IV fluid.
  2. Spike the bag, close the roller clamp, and prime the line using the pump’s prime function to remove air.
  3. Power on, select the correct profile/patient, open the pump door, and load the tubing following the arrows; close the door and clamps.
  4. Program the infusion (Rate mL/h + VTBI or Time); verify the soft limits and settings, then press Start.
  5. Open the roller clamp, confirm drip/chamber flow and the absence of alarms, and monitor the site for infiltration.

Common functions

  • Change rate/VTBI mid-infusion with the Rate or VTBI keys, confirming the updated values before resuming.
  • Pause/Start for line changes; pump will usually go to KVO when VTBI is reached.
  • Bolus (if enabled): hold the Bolus key and monitor the patient closely.
  • Silence alarms briefly while correcting issues; resolve the root cause before resuming.
  • Watch the battery indicator; plug in for extended infusions or when the battery icon drops low.

Troubleshooting

  • Occlusion alarm: Check for closed clamps, kinks, infiltrated IV, or the cassette not seated; verify bag height.
  • Air-in-line: Clamp the line, reprime to remove bubbles, and recheck the drip chamber level.
  • Door/cassette error: Reload tubing, ensure the anti-free-flow latch is engaged, and close the door firmly.
  • Battery low: Connect to AC and confirm the charge indicator illuminates.

Care & consumables

  • Use only Infusomat-compatible IV sets; change sets per medication policy.
  • Wipe exterior surfaces with approved disinfectant; keep the mechanism dry and free of residue.
  • Inspect the anti-free-flow feature and tubing tracks before use.
