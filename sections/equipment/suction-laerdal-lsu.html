Placeholder — Excessive suction pressure can cause mucosal trauma, bleeding, and vagal stimulation (bradycardia). Use appropriate pressure settings.

Placeholder — Prolonged suctioning causes hypoxia. Limit each attempt and pre-oxygenate between passes.

Placeholder — Handle aspirated material as infectious. Dispose of canisters as clinical waste.

Placeholder — Ensure bacterial filter is in place to protect the unit from contamination.

Placeholder — Keep suction unit assembled, charged, and immediately accessible at all times — suction may be needed urgently.
