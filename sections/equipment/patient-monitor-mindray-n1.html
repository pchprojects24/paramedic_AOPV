Placeholder — This monitor is not a defibrillator. Do not use for defibrillation or cardioversion. Use dedicated defibrillator (Propaq MD) for these functions.

Placeholder — Always verify alarm settings when beginning monitoring on a new patient. Do not rely on previous patient settings.

Placeholder — NIBP measurements may be inaccurate during excessive patient movement, arrhythmias, or in extremes of blood pressure.

Placeholder — The N1 is ideal for transport monitoring due to its compact size and long battery life. Pair with Propaq MD when defibrillation capability is also required.
